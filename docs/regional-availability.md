# Regional Availability

Seedance 2.0 Gateway Service is not available in the United States, India, Russia, and certain other regions due to upstream provider restrictions.

If Seedance 2.0 is unavailable in your region, alternative video models — including **Kling V3**, **Seedance 1.5 Pro**, and **Veo 3.1** — remain available through the same EvoLink API key.
